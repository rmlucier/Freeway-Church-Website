Your Next Steps

Ready to take the next step in your faith journey? Here's how to get started.

1. Salvation

Accept Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior. Salvation is a gift of God's grace received by faith.

"If you openly declare that Jesus is Lord... you will be saved." - Romans 10:9-10

2. Baptism

Water baptism and baptism of the Holy Spirit. Public declaration and spiritual empowerment.

"Go and make disciples... baptizing them..." - Matthew 28:19

3. Give

Tithing and Kingdom Builders. Worship God through generous giving and stewardship.

"Bring all the tithes into the storehouse..." - Malachi 3:10

4. Serve

Use your gifts and talents to build up the body of Christ and serve the community.

"God has given each of you a gift... Use them well." - 1 Peter 4:10

5. Membership

Commit to belong and partner in the mission and life of Freeway Church.

"Let us not neglect our meeting together..." - Hebrews 10:25
